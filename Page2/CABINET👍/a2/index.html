Features & details
A design with emphasis on form and function. The polygonal mesh design featuring a three-dimensional contour that’s not only aesthetical but is capable of simultaneously providing high airflow and dust filtration.
The crystalline design spans the edges of the tempered glass side panel, precision engineered for strength as well as aesthetics. It supports Mini-ITX, Micro-ATX, ATX, SSI CEB, E-ATX (up to 12” x 10.7” motherboards).
It can take up-to two 360mm radiators on the top and front panel to keep the most extreme systems cool enabling stable performance even during peak usage. Support for up to seven 120mm fans and 360mm front and top radiator and rear liquid cooling too.
With 2 x 3.5’’/2.5’’ Combo HDD and 4 (2 + 2) x 2.5’’ SSD drive bays. Supports up to a 410mm graphics card, a 165mm CPU cooler, and up to a 180mm PSU in length enabling room for upgrades. Also comes with 2 x USB 3.2 Gen 1 (USB 3.0), 1 x 3.5mm Audio Jack, 1 x 3.5mm Mic Jack , 1 x ARGB 2-to-3 Splitter.
Three pre-installed 120mm addressable RGB fans flood the Polygonal Mesh front panel with lighting while providing considerable airflow to the system. Certified compatible with: ASUS, Gigabyte, MSI and ASRock motherboards.
